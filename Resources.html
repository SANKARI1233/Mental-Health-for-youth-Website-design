Resources for Youth Mental Health

  1. National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Provides free, confidential support 24/7 for individuals in distress. Website
  2. The Trevor Project: Offers crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ+ youth. Website
  3. Crisis Text Line: Text-based support for those in crisis, available 24/7. Website
  4. NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness): Provides education, support, and advocacy for individuals and families affected by mental illness. Website
  5. Mental Health America: Offers resources, screening tools, and information on mental health conditions. Website
  6. Headspace: Provides guided meditation and mindfulness exercises to help manage stress and anxiety. Website
  7. Kids Help Phone: Canada's 24/7 support service for youth, offering counseling, information, and referrals. Website
  8. OK2Talk: Online community for teens and young adults struggling with mental health issues to share their stories and find support. Website
  9. Anxiety and Depression Association of America (ADAA): Offers information and resources on anxiety, depression, and related disorders. Website
  10. Teen Mental Health: Provides resources, tools, and information specifically designed for teenagers. Website
  11. Mind: UK-based mental health charity offering information, support, and advocacy. Website
  12. The Jed Foundation: Focuses on emotional health and preventing suicide among college and university students. Website
